# OHMployee Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

## Overview

OHMployee is an internal workforce operations and HR workflow platform used for employee management, vacancy processing, manpower monitoring, and operational coordination.

This Privacy Policy explains how OHMployee collects, stores, and protects operational and employee-related information.

---

## Information We Collect

OHMployee may collect and process:

- User account information
- Employee records
- Vacancy and staffing records
- HR Emploc processing data
- Plantilla and deployment records
- Mobile numbers and operational contact details
- Role and account/group access assignments
- Workflow approvals and operational activity logs
- Notification and alert preferences

---

## How Information Is Used

Information inside OHMployee is used strictly for:

- Workforce operations
- Vacancy management
- HR processing workflows
- Employee deployment tracking
- Manpower fill rate monitoring
- SLA monitoring and notifications
- Operational approvals and coordination
- Security and access control enforcement

OHMployee is intended for authorized company-internal use only.

---

## Authentication and Access Control

OHMployee uses role-based access control (RBAC) and scoped permissions to restrict access to operational and employee data.

Access may be limited by:

- User role
- Group assignment
- Account assignment
- Operational scope

Unauthorized access attempts may be logged and monitored.

---

## Data Storage and Security

OHMployee uses Supabase-hosted infrastructure and database services for secure data storage and authentication.

Security measures may include:

- Role-based access policies
- Row-level security (RLS)
- Authentication controls
- Session validation
- Scoped operational visibility

---

## Notifications

OHMployee may send operational notifications related to:

- Approval requests
- Workflow updates
- SLA alerts
- Correction requests
- Vacancy processing
- Operational announcements

---

## Data Sharing

OHMployee does not sell personal data.

Operational data is only accessible to authorized users based on organizational permissions and operational responsibilities.

---

## Data Retention

Operational and employee-related records may be retained based on company operational, compliance, and reporting requirements.

Archived records may remain securely stored for administrative and audit purposes.

---

## User Responsibilities

Authorized users are responsible for:

- Protecting login credentials
- Using OHMployee only for authorized purposes
- Preventing unauthorized data access
- Following company operational and security policies

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, security, or platform changes.
